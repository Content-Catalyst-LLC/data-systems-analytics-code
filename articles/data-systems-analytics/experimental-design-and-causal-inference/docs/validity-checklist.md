# Validity checklist

## Internal validity

- Randomization or identification strategy is credible.
- Treatment precedes outcome.
- Confounder adjustment avoids post-treatment variables.
- Attrition and missingness are assessed.
- Interference and spillovers are considered.
- Measurement error is reviewed.

## External validity

- Target population is defined.
- Study setting is compared with intended application setting.
- Heterogeneous treatment effects are considered.
- Transportability assumptions are stated.
