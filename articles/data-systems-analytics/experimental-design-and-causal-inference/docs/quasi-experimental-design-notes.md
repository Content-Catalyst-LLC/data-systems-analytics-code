# Quasi-experimental design notes

## Difference-in-differences

Requires a credible parallel-trends argument. Use pre-trends, placebo outcomes, sensitivity checks, and careful comparison-group selection.

## Regression discontinuity

Requires continuity of potential outcomes at the cutoff and no precise manipulation of the running variable. Use bandwidth sensitivity and density checks.

## Propensity weighting

Requires exchangeability conditional on observed covariates and positivity. It does not solve unobserved confounding.
