# Aggregation risk notes

Averages and totals are useful, but they can hide distributional structure.

Review:

- subgroup means and medians
- subgroup variance
- missingness by subgroup
- outlier concentration
- small-count categories
- period-specific shifts
- Simpson-type reversals
- pooled summaries that obscure segment-level decline

A descriptive report should show what the aggregate summarizes and what it hides.
