# BI governance checklist

- [ ] Metric definitions are certified where decision-critical.
- [ ] Dashboard owners are named.
- [ ] Access policies match sensitivity and role.
- [ ] Refresh expectations are documented.
- [ ] Lineage connects dashboards to metric logic and data sources.
- [ ] Alerts have response owners.
- [ ] Decision reviews capture whether evidence was used.
- [ ] Ad hoc exploration is distinguished from official reporting.
- [ ] Stale or duplicate dashboards are reviewed.
- [ ] BI usage informs product and metric improvement.
