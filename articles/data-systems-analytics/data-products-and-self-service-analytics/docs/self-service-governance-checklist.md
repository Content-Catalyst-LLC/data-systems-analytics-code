# Self-service governance checklist

Self-service analytics should widen trustworthy use, not multiply inconsistency.

- [ ] Users can discover certified products through a catalog.
- [ ] Product definitions include owner, domain, grain, freshness, and sensitivity.
- [ ] Certified semantic metrics are separated from ad hoc calculations.
- [ ] Access policies match data sensitivity.
- [ ] Quality status is visible to consumers.
- [ ] Lineage connects products to upstream sources and downstream dashboards.
- [ ] Decision-critical reporting uses certified products.
- [ ] Experimental analysis is clearly distinguished from certified reporting.
- [ ] Deprecated products are marked and retired.
- [ ] Analysts and business users receive interpretation guidance.
