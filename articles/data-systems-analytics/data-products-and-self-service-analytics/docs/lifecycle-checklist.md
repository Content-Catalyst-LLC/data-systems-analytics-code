# Data product lifecycle checklist

A product should not live forever by accident.

- [ ] Draft product has a named owner.
- [ ] Beta product has pilot consumers and feedback capture.
- [ ] Active product has quality checks and support expectations.
- [ ] Certified product has approved semantics and governance review.
- [ ] Product changes are versioned and communicated.
- [ ] Product usage is monitored.
- [ ] Stale products are reviewed.
- [ ] Deprecated products have replacement guidance.
- [ ] Retired products are archived according to retention policy.
